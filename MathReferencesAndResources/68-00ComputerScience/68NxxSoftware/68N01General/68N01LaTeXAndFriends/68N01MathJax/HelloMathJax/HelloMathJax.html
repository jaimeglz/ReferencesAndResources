    A formula

    Infrastructure as a service (IaaS) cloud computing annual divestment costs:

    $$ C_{aDvst(IaaS)} = { C_{aSftwMaint} + C_{aPrgrmrSpprt} + C_{aCustSpprt} + \sum_{i=1}^n {U_i F_i}. } $$ Where $C_{aDvst(IaaS)}$ represents total annual diverstment costs; $C_{aSftwMaint}$ is the annual cost of software maintenance, $C_{aPrfstnlSpprt}$ is the annual cost of professional support, $C_{aCustSpprt}$ is the annual cost of customer support, $U_i$ is the level of usage, and $F_i$ the usage fee.
